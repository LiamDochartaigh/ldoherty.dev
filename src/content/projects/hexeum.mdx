## About Project

Hexeum is a Twitch overlay marketplace that's been serving the streaming community since 2020. What began as a small project creating custom graphics for friends evolved into a comprehensive platform supporting over 300,000 content creators worldwide.

The project gained recognition through Streamlabs' Designer Spotlight program and has established itself as a leading provider of professional stream overlays, animated graphics, and customizable widgets. With over 6 years of expertise in the streaming industry, Hexeum offers hundreds of professionally designed templates spanning various streaming categories from esports to variety content.

The platform focuses on providing user-friendly, animated overlays that are compatible with industry-leading streaming software, helping content creators enhance their stream branding at affordable prices.
