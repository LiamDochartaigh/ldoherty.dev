## Etsy Bulk Sale Creator

Automates the Etsy sale creation flow. Upload a CSV file with your sale details and the extension fills every form field, selects your shop sections, and either stops at the review page for you to check, or confirms and creates all sales in sequence.

## Tech

- jQuery
- TypeScript
- CSS / HTML / JavaScript
